The Company

Hot Tub Superstore

Hot Tub Superstore (HTS) is a multi-location hot tub retailer operating three showrooms across the Phoenix, AZ metro area. With reps spread across three buildings, the sales manager physically could not be in more than one location at a time. Coaching happened informally — whoever the manager happened to be near might catch a quick tip — while the rest of the team sold in a blind spot. There was no way to systematically review sales conversations, compare performance across locations, or ensure every rep was getting coached.

The Challenge

One manager. Three locations. Zero visibility.

  • The sales manager could only physically be at one location at a time — leaving two locations uncoached every day
  • No way to compare rep performance across the three showrooms
  • Inconsistent coaching — feedback depended entirely on which store the manager happened to be in
  • No performance data — leadership couldn’t track which reps were improving or where the weak spots were
  • No visibility into what reps were actually saying to customers in any of the three locations
The Solution

Noetra — deployed in February 2026

HTS rolled out Noetra to their 5-person sales team across all three locations in February 2026. Every rep — no matter which showroom they worked out of — now captures their customer conversations directly from their phone. Noetra transcribes each conversation, separates speakers, and runs AI coaching analysis against HTS’s custom sales process. The sales manager suddenly had a window into every pitch, at every location, without ever leaving their desk.

How It Works

  1. Rep opens Noetra on their phone — taps Record before a customer walk-up, at any of the three showrooms
  2. Noetra transcribes the conversation with speaker diarization so each side of the dialogue is clearly labeled
  3. AI scores the interaction against HTS’s sales process — greeting, needs assessment, product knowledge, objection handling, close
  4. Manager dashboard shows team-wide trends, individual rep progress, per-location performance, and recordings needing attention
Key Findings

What HTS discovered

Coaching Visibility Across All 3 Locations

For the first time, the sales manager could see every customer interaction across every showroom — not just the ones at whichever store they were physically in. AI scoring surfaced specific moments where reps excelled or missed opportunities, regardless of location.

Per-Location Performance Tracking

The manager dashboard revealed clear differences between the three showrooms — which location was closing more consistently, where product knowledge was strongest, and which store needed hands-on attention. That intelligence was invisible before Noetra.

Rep-Level Coaching Insights

Some reps consistently scored high on greeting and rapport but low on closing. Others were strong closers but weak on needs assessment. For the first time, leadership could coach each rep to their specific weak spot instead of giving generic advice.

Zero Workflow Disruption

Reps record from their existing phones. No new hardware. No special setup. The 30-second habit of tapping Record before a customer walk-up was the only behavior change required — and it worked the same at every showroom.

“Before Noetra, I had to pick which store to be at. Now I can hear every pitch, at every showroom, and coach my team on specifics instead of guesses. It’s like being in three places at once.”

— Sales Manager, Hot Tub Superstore
Why It Matters

The multi-location showroom blind spot

Multi-location showroom retailers — hot tub dealers, furniture stores, auto dealerships, appliance retailers — all share the same structural problem:

  • High-value transactions ($5,000–$15,000+ per sale)
  • Consultative sales processes that take 20–60 minutes
  • Multiple showrooms with reps spread across them
  • A single sales manager who can only physically be in one place at a time
  • No existing call recording infrastructure (unlike phone-based sales)

Before Noetra, the only option was to hire more managers — or accept that most of your sales floor was selling unsupervised. Noetra closes the gap without adding headcount.
