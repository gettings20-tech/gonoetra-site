The Problem

Sales coaching is broken.
Here's why.

👥

Managers can't be in every conversation

The average sales manager oversees 8-12 reps. They witness maybe 2% of actual sales interactions. The other 98%? Completely uncoached.

🎲

Coaching is inconsistent

Different managers, different feedback. New hires get one style, veterans get another — or none at all. There's no standard, no system.

💸

Training doesn't stick

Companies spend $20B+ a year on sales training. Reps forget 87% within 30 days. One-off workshops don't change behavior — daily reinforcement does.
