Why Teams Switch

The differences that matter

🎯

Transcription accuracy you can actually trust

Coaching is only as good as the transcription it's based on. Multiple Rilla users have reported accuracy as low as 6-7% in real-world conditions — including one reviewer who said the app transcribed profanity into his opening greeting. Noetra uses Deepgram's latest Nova-3 engine, delivering significantly better accuracy even in noisy showroom environments.

💰

Transparent pricing with no minimums

Noetra starts at $79/rep/month with no minimum seats, no annual commitments, and no surprise fees. Rilla requires a sales conversation to get pricing and typically requires annual contracts. For a 10-person team, Noetra's transparent model means you know exactly what you'll pay before you sign up.

⚡

Reliable recording that stays in your control

Rilla users have reported the app starting recordings without being prompted, significant battery drain, and speaker identification that confuses the rep with the customer. Noetra's web-based recorder gives you full control — hit record when you want, stop when you're done.

🔧

Your sales process, your way

Noetra lets you fully customize your coaching framework — define the steps that matter for your business, weight them how you want, and evolve them over time. Whether you sell hot tubs, solar panels, or enterprise software, the AI coaches against your playbook.
